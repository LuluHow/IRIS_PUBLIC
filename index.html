Our Story

IRIS was born from a personal experience that changed our lives: our 4-year-old daughter's epilepsy diagnosis. Faced with constant anxiety and a feeling of helplessness, we decided to take action.

Let's be clear: we're not selling false hopes or miracle solutions. IRIS is simply the result of our determination as parents, developed entirely by ourselves. We're not a company with a marketing team or customer service - just two parents who created the tool we desperately needed.

Your feedback is essential to us. If you encounter any problems or have suggestions to improve IRIS, contact us directly. We personally respond to every message.

With you in this fight,
Ludovic and Tiffanie
